# Fix: "Service is Malfunctioning" Error

## Quick Fix Steps

### Step 1: Disable the Service
1. Go to **Settings** → **Accessibility**
2. Find **"Wire Auto Messenger"** in the list
3. **Toggle it OFF** (disable it)
4. Wait 5 seconds

### Step 2: Re-enable the Service
1. In the same Accessibility Settings
2. Find **"Wire Auto Messenger"** again
3. **Toggle it ON** (enable it)
4. Tap **"Allow"** or **"OK"** on any warning dialog

### Step 3: Restart the App
1. Close the **Wire Auto Messenger** app completely
2. Reopen the app
3. Check if the status shows as enabled (green checkmark)

---

## If Still Not Working

### Method 1: Uninstall and Reinstall
1. **Uninstall** Wire Auto Messenger app
2. **Restart** your phone
3. **Reinstall** the APK
4. **Enable** Accessibility Service again

### Method 2: Clear App Data
1. Go to **Settings** → **Apps** → **Wire Auto Messenger**
2. Tap **"Storage"**
3. Tap **"Clear Data"** and **"Clear Cache"**
4. **Restart** the app
5. **Re-enable** Accessibility Service

### Method 3: Check Device-Specific Settings (Redmi/Xiaomi)
1. Go to **Settings** → **Apps** → **Manage apps**
2. Find **"Restricted Settings"** app
3. Open it and ensure **"Wire Auto Messenger"** is **allowed**
4. Go back to **Accessibility Settings**
5. **Disable** and **Re-enable** the service

---

## Common Causes

1. **Service crashed during initialization** - Fixed by disabling/re-enabling
2. **Notification permission issues** - Service now handles this gracefully
3. **Device-specific restrictions** - Check Restricted Settings (Redmi)
4. **App was updated** - Re-enable service after update

---

## Prevention

After installing or updating the app:
- Always **disable and re-enable** the Accessibility Service once
- This ensures the service initializes properly
- The app will now handle errors better to prevent crashes

---

## Still Having Issues?

If the service still shows as malfunctioning:
1. Check Android version (requires Android 7.0+)
2. Ensure Wire app is installed
3. Try on a different device if possible
4. Check device logs for specific error messages
